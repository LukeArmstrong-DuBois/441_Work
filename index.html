Pressing Issue Today

I think that a very pressing issue that affects the world at large is the lack of understanding people have for one another.

If people were exposed to differing world views and stepped out of their echo chambers, they would see that our differences are far smaller than our similarities. This would not only make the world a more accepting and tolerant place but also make people think about fear-mongering techniques that do nothing more than build an "us vs. them" mindset.

I think that video games can help fix this problem by unifying players together so they are forced to interact with people from backgrounds and cultures different from their own.
